Bryan Singer, an incredible director, took the characters and spun the
story into something beautiful. Alan Cumming's 'NightCrawler' was a
revelation and the subtext of accepting homosexuality/being a latent
Mutant was beyond great. The coming out seen with iceman and his family
is phucking exceptional and Wolverine claiming he's their art teacher
was perfect comedy. The brian cox character was greatly cast as was the
ever so phucking beautiful Kelly Hu...The opening of this film set the tone and the movie took off running
and never once slowed down. Seeing Bryan and his crafty ways of
revealing characters from future films by threading them in was
seamless and eloquent. I am truly disappointed that Bryan's name is not
attached to the third, i feel as though superman is going to be a
superdud and he should've stuck with this.
