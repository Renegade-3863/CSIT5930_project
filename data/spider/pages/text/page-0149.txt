Uh...is there a plot here?, 11 February 2002
Author:
Bud Sturguess from Texas, USA
We've all been in the situation in which we wake up at 1:00 AM and decide
to
channel surf, and come across cheezy B-movies (many of them direct to
video)
on late-night cable. The films' scenarios are always the same--a degrading
graphic sexual situation every five minutes (and to fill the ten second
non-sexual voids, a shower scene), but the label of "pornography" is
pathetically covered with some weak plot (that is never followed, due to
the
heavy reliance on nudity). "Naked Lies" is no exception. To be honest with
you, I find nothing special about this film; I just got fed up with all of
the kinds of movies I just described and decided to bash the first one I
saw
on a late-night cable.
--Bud Sturguess, Texas, USA
