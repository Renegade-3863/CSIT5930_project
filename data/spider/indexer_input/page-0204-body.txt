Stylized show with some substance, 27 September 2003
Author:
Marc (marc_wrz) from My room.
At 13 episodes, SoulTaker is a short series and benefits from excellent
animation quality. The series is very cryptic up until the last few
episodes
and why many will end up confused after the first 2 volumes, I enjoyed
them
all. The characters are all fun and interesting and while this isn't the
best anime series around, give it a try, its got that goth/horror/sci-fi
elements mixed with the classic styled transformed-superhero action
popular
in the 80's.
