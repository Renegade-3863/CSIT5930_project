Nice DVD Cover, Bad Movie, 12 July 2003
Author:
kh98021 from United States
This is not a feature film, but a collection of 10 shorts.The total
running time is 70 minutes.All except the first are in French with
English subtitles.All are either directly or obliquely (sometimes
very obliquely indeed) about AIDS.None are exceptional.I have
been known to enjoy DVD collections of short gay-themed films.If
some of them are not very good, at least I enjoy the different
perspectives that filmmakers bring in their examination of what it
means to lead a gay life.For me, none of these films was
remotely interesting.If you are looking for some smart, sexy,
French short films, rent the collection of François Ozon's short
films that has been released to DVD.
