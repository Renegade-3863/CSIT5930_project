Trivia:
Dave Chappelle left the show abruptly during filming of the third season. Comedy Central had to pull the show from its 2005 schedule leaving the future of the show uncertain. The network decided to shelve the completed sketches hoping Chappelle would eventually return to finish the season. After Chappelle publicly announced that he had no intention of returning to the show, Comedy Central decided to repackage some of the completed sketches and air them in 2006. The sketches were presented minus the introductory wraparounds Chappelle used to do as host as those segments had not been taped before Chappelle left. Charles Q. Murphy and Donnell Rawlings took over the hosting duties.
