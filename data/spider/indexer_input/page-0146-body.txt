Tepid Ghost Story, 3 September 2000
Author:
phred22 from Maryland
The hero's name is John and that gives you a good idea of this film's lack
of imagination. Except for being black, this couple who move into a haunted
house are strictly by-the-numbers characters, as is what happens to them.
Special effects are primitive, without even camp value. There are sex
scenes
and nudity but nothing special about them. Our horny ghost is about as
interesting as a third-rate strip show. Only the current lack of other
description motivates me to write this.
